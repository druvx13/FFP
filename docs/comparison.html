Key Distinguishing Features of FFP

1. Patent Protection with Retaliation

FFP provides explicit patent grants while including retaliation provisions that terminate patent rights for aggressors. This is similar to Apache 2.0 but more comprehensive.

2. No Lockdown Provision

FFP explicitly prohibits imposing measures that prevent recipients from exercising their rights. This is unique among permissive licenses.

3. Cure Provisions

FFP allows reinstatement of rights if violations are cured, with a 60-day grace period. This is more generous than many licenses.

4. Broad Scope

FFP explicitly covers software, documentation, art, media, data, and hardware designs, making it versatile for various types of works.

When to Choose FFP

  • ✅ You want permissive licensing with patent protection
  • ✅ You need patent retaliation against trolls
  • ✅ You want to prohibit lockdown measures explicitly
  • ✅ You need broad coverage (software, docs, art, data, hardware)
  • ✅ You prefer clear definitions and terms
  • ✅ You want cure provisions for violations

License Compatibility

FFP works well with:

  • ✅ MIT License
  • ✅ BSD Licenses (2-Clause and 3-Clause)
  • ✅ Apache License 2.0
  • ⚠️ MPL 2.0 (with care)

Potential issues with:

  • ⚠️ GPL v2/v3 (incompatible due to different philosophies)
  • ⚠️ LGPL (similar issues)
  • ⚠️ AGPL (incompatible)
